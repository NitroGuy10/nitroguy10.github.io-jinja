Hi there!

I'm NitroGuy. Currently, I am a human who lives on Earth. My hobbies include programming, building circuits, playing video games, producing music, and sleeping.

I am highly interested in computer science and software development. At the moment, I am quite competent with Java but reeeeally need to branch out more. I've dabbled in JavaScript as well as some game engine scripting languages such as Unity-C# and Godot's GDScript. I've only had a little bit of experience with building simple circuits to manipulate with microcontrollers but I'm already in love.

Video games have been a massive part of my life but I find myself playing them less and less due to lack of time. Some of my all-time favorites are Rollercoaster Tycoon 2, Minecraft (of course), Factorio, Terraria, and Team Fortress 2. As of the time of writing this, I am currently playing a lot of osu! and Overwatch.

I am chronically addicted to music. I love playing, producing, and listening to music. I've loved a variety of genres over the years but, right now, I am most fixated on Dubstep and EDM. I have a very, very weird music brain. I started taking piano lessons at age 6 and was a pretty great student for the first couple of years. At a certain point, however, I kind of got sick of practicing. I would never practice but instead opt to doodle around and improvise or try to play songs I had heard on the radio by ear. I have perfect pitch and can transcribe and play by ear super easily and I consider myself very lucky for having that ability. I eventually quit lessons but, ironically, I started playing piano much more after that since I was free to doodle and compose without fear of my teacher scolding me for not learning my assigned music. I don't think that music education puts nearly as much emphasis on composition as it should. Music production quickly became one of my main hobbies and one of the things I feel happiest doing. I'm much happier being a music producer than a pianist.

I produce EDM under the alias "SassyMonkey".
